What is GST?

GST stands for Goods and Services Tax, it is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. It subsumes various indirect taxes such as VAT, Excise, and Service Tax and replaces them with a single tax. The GST is governed by the GST Council and is levied at multiple rates ranging from 0% to 28% based on the nature of goods and services.

GST is a single indirect tax in India on goods & services, replacing previous taxes. The tax rate varies 0-28% and is controlled by the GST Council.

GST Calculation Formula

In order to add GST to base amount


GST Amount = ( Original Cost * GST% ) / 100
Net Price = Original Cost + GST Amount

In order to remove GST from base amount


GST Amount = Original Cost – (Original Cost * (100 / (100 + GST% ) ) )
Net Price = Original Cost – GST Amount

What is Inclusive GST?

Inclusive GST refers to a price that already includes the GST amount, so the buyer sees the final price including the tax.

What is Exclusive GST?

Exclusive GST refers to a price that does not include the GST amount, so the buyer needs to pay the tax separately on top of the listed price.
